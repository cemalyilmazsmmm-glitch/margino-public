FAQ

Questions, answered.

Where does my data go?

Into a Google Sheet that you own. Margino creates the Sheet on your Drive and writes your parsed receipts there. You can open, edit, export, or delete it anytime — and Margino only ever touches the file it created.

How are the platform prices calculated?

Margino starts from your real unit cost and works up to a price that holds your target margin after typical delivery-platform commissions, so your in-store, UberEats, and DoorDash prices each stay profitable.

Do I need to type anything?

No. Snap a photo of the receipt or invoice and Margino reads the line items for you. You get a quick review screen to confirm before it syncs.

What does it cost to start?

It's free to start — 10 receipts a month, no credit card. Upgrade in-app when you need more volume.

Which devices are supported?

Margino is an iPhone app, available now on the App Store in 175 countries.
